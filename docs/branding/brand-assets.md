# Brand assets

## Phase 05 provider presentation

Phase 05 uses neutral Material provider icons for Bank of Palestine, Jawwal Pay, and PalPay. They are intentionally not brand-logo substitutes and do not make a claim of affiliation.

No official reusable raster or vector asset was added in this phase because a suitable asset and its permitted reuse terms were not verified from an official organization source during implementation. The application does not hotlink provider assets and does not request Internet permission.
